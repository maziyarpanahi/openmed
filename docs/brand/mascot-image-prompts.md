# OpenMed Mascot — AI Image Prompts (ChatGPT / GPT-image)

Full, copy-paste prompts for each mascot direction. **The Persian Cat (Avicenna-inspired)
is the personal front-runner.** **Generate one at a time** (GPT-image makes a single image
per prompt). All share OpenMed's palette and the "guardian of your data" concept. See
[`MASCOT_BRIEF.md`](MASCOT_BRIEF.md) for the full brief.

---

## ⭐ Featured · Persian Cat — Avicenna-inspired 🐱

> Heritage: **Avicenna (Ibn Sina)**, the great Persian physician whose *Canon of Medicine*
> was the world's standard medical text for ~600 years → symbol = the **Canon of Medicine**
> (open book). Bonus: **turquoise (fīrūza)** is the Persian national gem and ≈ OpenMed's teal,
> so **Persian arabesque tilework** accents are perfectly on-brand.

```
Create a friendly mascot logo of a fluffy white Persian cat as the gentle guardian-physician
of your private health data — inspired by Avicenna (Ibn Sina), the great Persian doctor who
wrote "The Canon of Medicine."

The plump, long-haired Persian cat sits upright with a warm, calm, kind expression, a flat
round face, and big gentle eyes. It wears a small traditional Persian scholar's turban (in the
style of Avicenna) and a slim collar with a small round tag showing a coral-red medical plus
(+). In front of the cat lies a simple open book — a stylized "Canon of Medicine" — with a
bold coral-red medical plus (+) on the page and a soft warm-yellow glow rising from it, like
precious knowledge. A subtle Persian turquoise arabesque (tilework) pattern decorates the
book's cover.

Beneath the character, the wordmark "OpenMed" in a clean bold modern sans-serif — "Open" in
dark ink and "Med" in teal. Spell it exactly "OpenMed".

Style: modern flat vector mascot/logo illustration, bold clean dark outlines, simple rounded
shapes, stylized shape-based fluffy fur (not individual strands), minimal flat shading,
friendly proportions; the polished, ownable feel of a great open-source mascot.

Color palette (stick to these): cream / off-white fur (#F7F4EC) with soft deep-teal accents on
the ears and tail tip (#0D6E6E to #128787); a teal turban and collar; the medical plus and
small accents in coral red (#C5453A); a Persian-turquoise (#128787) arabesque pattern; dark ink
outlines (#0E1116); a soft warm-yellow glow (#F5E27A), used sparingly.

Composition: a single character, centered, with generous empty margins, on a solid flat
off-white (#F7F4EC) background — no extra scenery, uncluttered. Square 1:1.

Avoid: realistic photographic fur; any real Red Cross emblem (use only the coral plus); national
flags or politically charged emblems; gradients, 3D rendering, or photorealism; busy
backgrounds; menacing looks; any text other than the word "OpenMed" (spelled correctly).
```

## Tips for getting good results

- **One prompt → one image.** Run them, compare, then iterate on the favorite.
- **If it drifts 3D or off-color**, reply: *"Flatter, more limited palette — just deep
  teal, cream, and coral. Bold flat vector, no gradients, no 3D."*
- **Persian Cat — watch the wordmark.** GPT-image often misspells text. If "OpenMed" comes
  out wrong, regenerate, or generate the cat clean and we set the wordmark in code. Also
  keep it uncluttered: *"Simplify — just the cat, the open book, and the OpenMed word."*
- **If the Saint Bernard comes out brown**, reply: *"Recolor the patches to deep teal
  (#0D6E6E), keep the rest cream — no brown."*
- **For the small app icon/favicon** (after you like one): *"Now show just the head/face
  as a simple app icon that stays clear at small sizes — same colors, flat vector."*
- **For the README hero banner:** *"Place this character on the left and the word
  'OpenMed' in a bold modern sans-serif on the right ('Med' in teal), wide 16:9 banner,
  cream background."*
- **For a clean cutout:** *"Same mascot on a plain solid white background"* → easy to
  remove the background later.
- **Exact hex isn't guaranteed**, but naming both the hex and the color word (done above)
  keeps it close. Ask for *"3 variations of this pose"* to get options.
