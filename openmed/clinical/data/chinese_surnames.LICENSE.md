# Chinese surname gazetteer provenance and license

SPDX-License-Identifier: CC0-1.0

`chinese_surnames.txt` contains only surname vocabulary. Its single-character
inventory is a simplified-Chinese normalization of surnames represented in the
anonymous Song-dynasty *Baijiaxing* (百家姓, “Hundred Family Surnames”)
tradition. Its two-character entries are common compound surnames from the same
public-domain cultural vocabulary.

The underlying historical text is public domain. OpenMed dedicates this
normalized factual compilation to the public domain under
[CC0 1.0 Universal](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).
The file contains no full names, clinical text, or real patient data.
